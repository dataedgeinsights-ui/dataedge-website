Section 01
Programme Overview and Eligibility

The WLGSM is a free, self-paced online certificate programme offered by DataEdge Insights Ghana. The programme comprises four weeks of structured content covering women's leadership, good governance, and SDG 5 achievement, culminating in a final assessment and Certificate of Completion.

The programme is open to:

  • Government officials, civil servants, and public sector professionals
  • NGO and civil society practitioners
  • Researchers, consultants, and M&E specialists
  • Students and academics with an interest in gender, governance, and development
  • Any individual committed to advancing gender equality and good governance

There are no academic prerequisites. Participants must be 18 years of age or older. By registering, you confirm that the information you provide is accurate, complete, and your own.

Section 03
Programme Structure and Completion Requirements

To receive a Certificate of Completion, participants must fulfil all of the following requirements in sequence:

  • All lessons in each week must be individually marked as complete before proceeding to the next lesson.
  • Module Reflection for each week must be completed. Reflections require a minimum of 10 words and a maximum of 200 words. Each reflection must be submitted and marked complete before the corresponding quiz becomes accessible.
  • Weekly Quizzes: Each of the four weekly quizzes must be passed with a minimum score of 80%. Passing a week's quiz is required before the following week's content is unlocked. Participants may retake quizzes. A 4-hour lockout applies after two consecutive failed attempts.
  • Final Assessment: The 10-question final assessment (45 minutes, 80% pass mark) becomes accessible only after all four weekly quizzes have been passed. Participants may retake the final assessment as many times as needed.

Progress is saved automatically in your browser's local storage. DataEdge Insights Ghana is not liable for loss of progress due to browser data being cleared, use of private/incognito browsing, or device changes. Participants are advised to complete the programme on the same device and browser.

Section 04
Certificate of Completion

Upon passing the final assessment with 80% or above, participants receive a Certificate of Completion bearing:

  • The participant's registered full name
  • The programme title: Women in Leadership, Good Governance and Sustainable Development Goal 5 Model (WLGSM)
  • The final assessment score achieved
  • A unique Certificate ID in the format DEI-WLGSM-GH-[YEAR]-[CODE]
  • The signature of Efua K. Turkson (PhD), Managing Director, DataEdge Insights Ghana
  • The issue date and verification URL: www.dataedgeinsights.org

The certificate is available for download as a PDF and for printing directly from the platform at any time after it is earned. It is permanently accessible within your account.

Certificate Validity: The certificate is valid indefinitely as a record of programme completion. DataEdge Insights Ghana does not guarantee that the certificate will be recognised by any employer, academic institution, or regulatory body. Participants are responsible for understanding how the certificate may be used or recognised in their own professional or academic context.

The certificate name is generated from the name provided at registration. Participants are responsible for ensuring their name is entered correctly. DataEdge Insights Ghana does not reissue certificates to correct names after they have been generated, except at its sole discretion.
