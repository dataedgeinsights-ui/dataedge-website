Section 6
Certification

6.1 Completion Requirements

To be awarded the DataEdge Insights Ghana YLIM Practitioner Certificate, Participants must complete at least 80% of all course lessons, submit all required reflection tasks and exercises, pass the final assessment with a score of 70% or above, and (for the Facilitator track) pass the assessed teach-back submission.

6.2 Re-assessment

Participants who do not pass the final assessment on their first attempt may re-take it up to twice within 20 days at no additional charge. A third failed attempt will require the Participant to re-enrol at the current fee if they wish to be assessed again.

6.3 Certificate Validity

The YLIM Practitioner Certificate is issued in digital format and is valid indefinitely from the date of issue. DataEdge does not guarantee that the certificate will be recognised by any specific employer, institution, or regulatory body.

6.4 Revocation of Certificate

DataEdge reserves the right to revoke a certificate where it is found that a Participant obtained it through dishonest means, including academic misconduct, sharing answers, or misrepresentation. Revocation will be communicated in writing and is final.

8.1 Eligibility

The Trainer Licence is available exclusively to Participants who have completed the Facilitator Track of the Programme and been awarded a certificate. 8.2 Licence Scope

A Trainer Licence grants the holder the right to deliver YLIM-based training to external participants under the DataEdge brand framework, charge fees for such delivery, and use DataEdge-approved training materials provided with the licence. It does not grant the right to modify the core YLIM methodology, issue certificates on behalf of DataEdge, or sub-licence to other trainers.

8.3 Annual Renewal

The Trainer Licence is valid for 12 months from the date of activation and must be renewed annually at the published rate (currently GHS 2,000 per year). Failure to renew will result in the licence lapsing. Delivery of YLIM training with a lapsed licence is a breach of these Terms.

Licensed trainers are required to maintain the quality and integrity of YLIM delivery. DataEdge reserves the right to audit or observe training delivered under licence and to withdraw the licence where delivery is found to be materially inconsistent with the YLIM framework or DataEdge's standards.
